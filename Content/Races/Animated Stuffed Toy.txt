## Animated Stuffed Toy
[**Source:** *(Tiny Creatures)*](https://drive.google.com/file/d/0ByMS1MkBFnXIalk2SFB5QnQzWWc/view)

**Note:** *Bear in mind the rules for being Tiny on the first page of the source.*

Most stuffed toys are just soft bags filled with stuffing, but some are something more. Occasionally a toy will gain sentience though being possessed, being animated by magic, or through a spirit from another dimension.

These animated toys can have any personality, but many seem to take on personalities similar to those of the children that owned them. Some stay With their children, but some go out into the world and seek their fortune.

### Stuffed Toy Names
Most stuffed toys keep the names given to them by the children that owned them, but some take new names. The following names are ideas of what your child might call you, if you pick your own it could be anything.

**Stuffed Toy Names:** Mr. Bears, Cuddles, Tutu, Tweety, Fluffles, Sylvester, Snowy, Fizz, Nibbles, Sparkle.

### Stuffed Toy Racial Traits
***Ability Score Increase.*** Your Charisma score increases by 2, and your Dexterity score increases by 1.

***Age.*** Due to their varied nature stuffed toys have many life spans. Most will outlive a human.

***Alignment.*** Stuffed toys come in all alignments, but many are neutral.

***Size.*** You are the size of a normal stuffed toy animal. Your size is tiny.

***Speed.*** Due to your tiny size, your base walking speed is 25 feet.

***Darkvision.*** You're accustomed to the darkness of children's rooms. You have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

***Stuffed Body.*** You gain resistance to bludgeoning damage and immunity to falling damage. However, you gain vulnerability to fire damage.

***Construct.*** You do not need to eat, sleep or breath, and any spells or effects that would affect a construct affect you.

***False Appearance.*** While you remain motionally, you are indistinguishable from a normal stuffed toy.

***Cute Demeanor.*** You have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks, but disadvantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks.

***Natural Stealthy.*** You gain proficiency with the Stealth skill.

***Languages.*** You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language, typically one your owner knew.
