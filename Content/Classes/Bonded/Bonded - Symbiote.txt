### The Symbiote
#### Symbiotic Form
Beginning at 3rd level, you learn to shroud yourself in the essense of your partner. As a bonus action, you may activate your symbiotic form. Your Symbiotic Form lasts for one minuite, until you become incapacitated, or until you end it on your turn as a free action. While your Symbiotic Form is Active you gain the following Benefits:
- Your Armor class becomes 10 + your dexterity mod + your constitution modifier unless it was already higher. You gain no benefit from any armor you were wearing.
- You gain a bonus to damage rolls equal to your proficiency bonus

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses of it when you finish a long rest.

#### Watch your back!
Beginning at 7th level, your bound companion has begun to look out for you and spot danger. You have advantage on initiative rolls and gain tremorsense out to 10ft.

#### Eldritch Protection
Beginning at 14th level, whenever you take damage, including damage from mutation points, while your Symbiotic form is up, you can use your reaction to instead take half damage.

#### Two Minds One Form
Beginning at 18th level, when your Symbiotic Form is activated, your mind merges with your Bound Companion. Whenever you roll a d20 while in your Symbiotic Form, you gain a bonus equal to 1d8.
