### Way of the Goodberry
[**Source:** *Praise Be The Goodberry! Compendium*](https://www.gmbinder.com/share/-LZeyRZtzKZLu2cjiglX)

There are those who see the Goodberry as a force with which they can better themselves. Monks of this sort seek to make themselves one with the Great Goodberry, both physically and spiritually.

#### Grasping Touch
At 3rd level, whenever you make an unarmed attack you can as a reaction cause vines to sprout from your hands and make a grapple check against the creature you attacked.

#### Knowledge of the Great Goodberry
At 6th level, you gain proficiency with the nature and survival skills if you aren't already proficient with them. Whenever you make a nature check you can use Wisdom instead.

#### Goodberry Tendrils
At 11th level, you may spend 1 ki point to increase the reach of your unarmed attacks by 10 feet for 1 minute. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier per long rest.

#### Goodberry Ascendant
At 17th level, you have become one with the Great Goodberry in mind, soul, and body. You can cast Kevin's Faithful Goodberry once per long rest without needing to expend a spell slot.
