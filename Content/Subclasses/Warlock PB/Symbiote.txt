#### Pact of the Symbiote
[**Source:** *The Symbiote*](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1pM516QRjD9oH1Sc8iUX9MdZZv7b8i9zP/view)

Your patron enhances physical aspects of your form to ensure its survival through symbiosis. You gain proficiency in both Athletics and Stealth checks. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for checks using these skills.

Additionally you no longer require to eat or drink. Both forms of sustenance are provided for you from your symbiotic relationship.
